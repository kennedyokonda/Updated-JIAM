GLORY TWIN TOWERS



"It takes time to grow up and become who you really are"and the journey has not been different for JIAM,a journey of step by step growth according to the will of God and as our Apostolic Bishop Margaret Wanjiru narrates ,it started by preaching at the streets and markets and by God's grace today JIAM ministry hosts 10,000 christians every Sunday .However , as our congregation exponentially grows, we are limited by space and When the GLORY TWINS TOWER (GTT) Vision came , we never hesitated in implementing it .A storey church building that will be able to host 25,000 christians per service ,and as we speak we are laying foundation for the first tower.Let's take a moment and reflect at the word of God which says "When you build God's house, God will build yours", We urge donors, wellwishers, christians of goodwill , Charity organisations and individuals to donate any amount that will make this project come to accomplishment and Almighty God will not forsake you in your needs and dwellings ,remember the hand that giveth is the hand that receiveth.Kindly click the SUPPORT GTT button below an donate any amount that will make this dream project come into reality.Thank you and indeed THE GLORY IS HERE
